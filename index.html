I am a postdoc researcher at the Laboratory for Statistical & Translational Genomics, which is led by Dr. Mingyao Li. It is a pleasure to work in this awesome team. Previously, I obtained my PhD degree in Computer Science and Informatics at Emory University advised by Dr. Hao Wu. My research concentrates on biostatistics/bioinformatics fields.

I am interested in designing machine (statistical) learning and deep learning methods for studying sequence data including (but not limited to) spatial transcriptomics, BS-seq, scRNA-seq, and scATAC-seq.
